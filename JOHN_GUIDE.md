# Managing your websites — a plain guide

You have two websites, and you can update both yourself, no code, no calling me for every little change.

- **Cole's Capital Group** (the company site): colescapitalgroup.com
- **Pines & Ponies** (the rentals site): pinesandponies.com

There are two places you'll manage things:

1. **The website admin** — for text, photos, projects, press, and your rental listings.
2. **OwnerRez** — for live bookings, calendars, and prices once the rentals are taking guests (we set this up when your properties are ready).

---

## Logging in to the admin

- Cole's site: go to **colescapitalgroup.com/admin**
- Pines site: go to **pinesandponies.com/admin**

Click **Login** and sign in. (I'll send you your login once the sites are on their real domains.) That's it, you'll see a simple dashboard.

---

## Pines & Ponies: adding or editing a rental

1. Log in to the Pines admin.
2. Click **Properties**.
3. To change one, click it. To add a new one, click **New Property**.
4. Fill in the boxes: name, nightly rate, beds, baths, photos, amenities, description.
   - **URL slug**: just lowercase words with dashes, like `pine-ridge`. This becomes the page web address.
   - **Photos**: click the photo box and upload from your computer. Drag to reorder the gallery.
5. Click **Publish**. The site rebuilds itself in about a minute, and your new rental shows up on the home page and gets its own page automatically.

Skip anything marked "optional", the site fills those in for you (page titles, search descriptions, all the technical stuff). Photos straight off your phone are fine, the site automatically shrinks them so pages stay fast. The admin works great from your phone, too.

That's the whole thing. Add a cabin, it appears. Change a price, it updates everywhere.

You can also click **Site Settings** to change your contact email, phone, the "Opening 2026" banner, and social links.

## Cole's Capital Group: editing the company site

Log in to the Cole's admin. You'll see sections you can manage:

- **Track Record** — your completed projects (name, year, category, note).
- **Pipeline** — upcoming projects (with a status like "Under Contract" or "Delivering 2027").
- **Press** — your Albany Business Review articles (headline + link).
- **Portfolio Classes** — the four asset-type cards.
- **Headline Stats** — the big numbers under the top of the page.
- **Site Settings** — contact info and social links.

Click any item to edit, or use **New** to add one. Hit **Publish** and the site updates itself.

## A few good-to-knows

- **Nothing breaks if you experiment.** Every change is saved with a history, so anything can be undone.
- **Photos**: clear, landscape photos look best. The site automatically makes them load fast.
- **Booking requests work today**: when a guest picks dates and hits "Request to Book," it emails you their name, email, dates, and the quoted total. You reply and confirm. When your properties are ready for real-time booking and payments, we connect OwnerRez and your Airbnb/Vrbo calendars stay in sync automatically.
- **Furnished Finder**: it doesn't connect to anything automatically (that's just how their site works), so you'll keep managing those dates by hand.
